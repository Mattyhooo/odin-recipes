Lasagne Recipe

The Best Lasagna

This classic lasagna is made with an easy meat sauce as the base. Layer the sauce with noodles and cheese,
then bake until bubbly! This is great for feeding a big family, and freezes well, too.

Ingredients

  • meat
  • pasta
  • cheese
  • sauce

Steps

  1. Cook Meat
  2. Mix Sauce
  3. Cook pasta
  4. Cook in oven
